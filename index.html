Methodology

How the data was processed

Data source

Official BAA results — Boston 2023 and 2025. Each runner has official splits at 5K, 10K, 15K, 20K, Half, 25K, 30K, 35K, 40K, Finish.

Filters

Buckets of ±2:30 around target time. Excluded: missing key splits. 2024 excluded: anomalous weather caused nearly double positive splits vs 2023.

Segment calculation

Seg.1=0–10K. Seg.2=10K–25K. Seg.3=25K–35K. Seg.4=35K–Finish. Deviation % = (seg pace − avg pace) / avg pace × 100.

Why exclude 2024?

In 2024, warm temperatures and headwinds caused positive splits nearly double vs 2023 (e.g. 2:50 men: +2:49 in 2023 vs +6:23 in 2024). Including 2024 distorts the model for normal conditions.
